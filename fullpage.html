Beverages

From the standard through to the spectacular, the team at Toplum have handpicked a selection of beverages designed to delight the palette.

We believe in quality, and that first impressions count – and that usually starts with a sip on one of our Toplum specials. A creamy Butter Scotch Latte, a Oatley Milk Chai, or a refreshing Melona or a Brazilian Lemonade.

We have the full selection of coffees, and each can be reimagined with Oatley, coconut, or almond milk. We have tea and iced tea aplenTEA, and mocktails and juices for a bit of zesty.
